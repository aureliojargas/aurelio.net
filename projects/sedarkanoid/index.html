Following the SedSokoban fever, here's the classic Arkanoid game implemented using the good old UNIX Stream EDitor. You know, SED.

Detailed instructions are available on the script's headers.

Note: Different from the original game, this one's not funny to play or even exciting. It's cool because it's SED.
